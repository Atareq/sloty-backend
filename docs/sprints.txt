Critical Team Lead Warnings
1. Do not start with marketplace

Still rejected for MVP 1.

You need reliable court data first.

2. Do not build payment gateway now

Manual transaction recording is enough.

Gateway comes after operations are validated.

3. Do not skip audit logs

Audit logs are core business value, not a luxury.

4. Do not allow raw money edits

Transactions must be voided/corrected, not edited.

5. Do not build a desktop-first UI

The real users are staff on phones.

Mobile-first is mandatory.

6. Do not overbuild pricing

Default court price + owner override is enough.

Advanced pricing engine later.

7. Do not ignore concurrency

Double booking is one of the most dangerous bugs.

Backend must protect this, not frontend only.

Suggested Development Order

The safest order:

Auth and roles
Clubs/courts
Staff assignments
Booking creation
Overlap protection
Transactions
Booking lifecycle
Settlement
Dashboards
Audit logs
QA/deployment

Do not build settlement before transactions.
Do not build bookings before courts.
Do not build dashboards before core data is stable.
