2. Final Product Context

The product will start as a free court management system for local football courts in Assiut.

The first goal is:

Replace paper booking and manual records inside local courts.

The future goal is:

Build a platform where players can book available slots through the application, while the platform earns commission from bookings it generates.

4. Core Product Principle

This rule is now considered a main project law:

Bookings can change until completed. Transactions can accumulate until settled. Completed bookings and settled transactions become locked.

This principle controls the entire system.

Meaning:

A booking can be edited/rescheduled/cancelled before completion.
Transactions can be added before settlement.
Transactions are not silently edited.
Completed bookings cannot be changed.
Settled transactions cannot be changed.
Corrections must be logged.

13. Booking Statuses

Final statuses:

Status	Meaning
Hold	Slot is reserved but no valid transaction exists
Confirmed	At least one valid transaction exists
Completed	Customer played and booking is locked
Cancelled	Booking was cancelled
No-show	Customer did not come
Expired	Hold expired without transaction
14. Booking Confirmation Rule

Final rule:

A booking starts as Hold if there is no transaction. It becomes Confirmed only after at least one valid transaction is attached.

So:

Booking without transaction → Hold
Booking with valid transaction → Confirmed

If all valid transactions are cancelled:

Confirmed → Hold
15. Slot Blocking Rule

Hold bookings block the slot.

Confirmed bookings block the slot.

Non-blocking statuses:

Cancelled
Expired
Completed in the past
No-show after closure

Important final rule:

Hold blocks the slot until it becomes Confirmed, Cancelled, or Expired.

16. Hold Expiry

For MVP 1:

Internal hold expires after 12 hours.

When hold expires:

Booking status becomes Expired.
Slot becomes available.
Booking remains in history.
Booking is not deleted.

Future:

Owner may configure expiry time.
Platform bookings may have shorter expiry such as 15 minutes.
17. Overlap Rule

The system must prevent overlapping active bookings on the same court.

Existing booking:

8:00 PM → 9:00 PM

Blocked:

8:30 PM → 9:30 PM

Allowed:

9:00 PM → 10:00 PM

Rule:

A new booking is not allowed if it overlaps with any Hold or Confirmed booking on the same court.

18. Booking Duration Rule

For MVP:

Booking follows court slot duration.
Multiple consecutive slots may be booked as one longer booking.

Example:

If slot duration is 60 minutes:

8–9 is one slot.
8–10 is two slots in one booking.

Final correction from user:

Final accepted rule:

54. Final BA/UX Recommendations
Recommendation 1

Keep MVP 1 focused on internal court management.

Do not add marketplace, payment gateway, or player app yet.

Recommendation 2

Make booking creation extremely fast.

The first version must be faster than paper.

Recommendation 3

Use transactions instead of a simple deposit field.

This is the correct decision because deposits can be paid multiple times and settlement depends on who collected the money.

Recommendation 4

Keep remaining amount dynamic.

Do not store remaining amount as a manually editable field.

Recommendation 5

Protect money records.

No raw transaction edits.
Use cancel/correction flow.

Recommendation 6

Protect completed bookings.

Completed bookings should be locked.

Recommendation 7

Protect settled transactions.

Settled transactions should be locked.

Recommendation 8

Build audit logs from day one.

This is not optional because the product promises security from manipulation.

Recommendation 9

Use staff shift settlement as a core MVP feature.

This is one of the strongest business values for owners.

Recommendation 10

Avoid overengineering permissions in MVP.

Staff belongs to one court.
Manager belongs to one club.
Owner owns clubs.
That is enough now.

Phase 2 is now complete.

A mobile-first Arabic court management system where Platform Admin creates clubs/courts, owners manage their clubs, managers manage club operations, staff manage one court, bookings start as Hold until payment is recorded, transactions accumulate dynamically, owner settles staff money, completed bookings are locked, settled transactions are locked, and all important actions are audit logged.
